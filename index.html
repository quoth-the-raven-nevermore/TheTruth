'Worth'less Friends

  • Cold is my destiny, colder was the day,
  • When my foes caused me this great dismay.
  • They succeeded in turning their friend into a foe,
  • Into a devil unknown, Into a fiend alone.
  • And oh, believe me, it is another of your countless mistakes,
  • For in truth, you have taunted a sleeping lion,
  • Who now roars for prey.

  • My heart grows colder with each passing day,
  • As all the hatred you have shown me, must be repaid.
  • But instead, I'll make you pay, and revenge will be mine,
  • Let the forces fight and come what may.
  • My heart is heavy, my soul been permanently scarred.
  • But now, comes the lion,
  • Don't let down your guard.

  • How different are our paths, we have had to follow,
  • How we used to be best friends,
  • And now you are the sole reason of my sorrow.
  • Destiny, you may call it,
  • Or simply my dumb luck,
  • My friends who called themselves worthy,
  • With their actions had left me dumbstruck.
  • But, Oh I'm not one of those who simply slop and cry,
  • On the friends who are gone,
  • On friends who left me to suffer and die.
  • These friends of mine left me in the hour of need,
  • Leaving me in times of sorrow, leaving me in grief.
  • Even heartbroken I will move on with life,
  • Moving on with new strength, courage and restore my damaged pride.
  • Oh, my dearest friends I don’t seek forgiveness,
  • All this while I couldn’t tell you your presence was worth the witness.
  • Though we used to be friends,
  • Time has turned us into enemies.
  • I miss the happy times,
  • As much as I hate the sorrowful days,
  • You all were good friends of mine,
  • While I only caused you trouble and pain.
